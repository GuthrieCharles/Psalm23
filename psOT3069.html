OT:3069 Yehovih (yeh-ho-vee');

A variation of OT:3068 [used after OT:136, and pronounced by Jews as OT:430, in order to prevent the repetition of the same sound, since they elsewhere pronounce OT:3068 as OT:136]:

KJV - God.
