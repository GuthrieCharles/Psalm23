OT:4616 ma`an (mah'-an);

From OT:6030; properly, heed, i.e. purpose; used only adverbially, on account of (as a motive or an aim), teleologically, in order that:

KJV - because of, to the end (intent) that, for (to, ... 's sake), lest, that, to.
