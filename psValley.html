OT:1516 gay' (gah'-ee); or (shortened) gay (gah'-ee);

Probably (by transmutation) from the same root as OT:1466
(abbreviated); a gorge (from its lofty sides; hence, narrow, but not a gully or winter-torrent):

KJV - valley.
