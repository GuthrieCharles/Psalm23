OT:6664 tsedeq (tseh'-dek); from OT:6663;

The right (natural, moral or legal); also (abstractly) equity or (figuratively) prosperity:

KJV - even, (X that which is altogether) just (-ice), ([un-]) right (-eous) (cause, -ly, -ness).
