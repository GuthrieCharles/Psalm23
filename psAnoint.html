OT:1878 dashen (daw-shane');

A primitive root; to be fat; transitively, to fatten (or regard as fat); specifically to anoint; figuratively, to satisfy; denominatively (from OT:1880) to remove (fat) ashes (of sacrifices): -accept, anoint, take away the (receive) ashes (from), make (wax) fat.
