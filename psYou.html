OT:859 'attah (at-taw');

Or (shortened); 'atta (at-taw'); or 'ath (ath); feminine (irregular) sometimes 'attiy (at-tee'); plural masculine 'attem (attem'); feminine 'atten (at-ten'); or 'attenah (at-tay'naw); or 'attennah (at-tane'-naw); a primitive pronoun of the second person; thou and thee, or (plural) ye and you:

KJV - thee, thou, ye, you.
