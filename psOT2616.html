OT:2616 chacad (khaw-sad');

A primitive root; properly, perhaps to bow (the neck only [compare OT:2603] in courtesy to an equal), i.e. to be kind; also (by euphem. [compare l OT:88], but rarely) to reprove:

KJV - shewself merciful, put to shame.
