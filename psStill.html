OT:4496 menuwchah (men-oo-khaw'); or menuchah (men-oo-khaw');

feminine of OT:4495; repose or (adverbially) peacefully; figuratively, consolation (specifically, matrimony); hence (concretely) an abode:

KJV - comfortable, ease, quiet, rest (-ing place), still.
